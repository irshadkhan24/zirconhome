ABOUT

Zircon Home

Established in 2010 by Mr. Wasim Khan & Mr. Akhtar Khan, Zircon Home is a self-built, professionally operated construction company rooted in hard work, dedication, and excellence.

Our credibility is strengthened by collaborations with leading developers, architects, and consultants across India. Clients trust Zircon Home for safety, sustainability, and timely delivery.

We specialize in civil construction, interior finishing, structural work, renovations, and full turnkey execution—driven by a skilled team and strict adherence to standards.

At Zircon Home, transparency, quality control, and innovation guide every step of our project execution.
